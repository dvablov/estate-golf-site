Support

Help for Estate Golf on iPhone.

Nearby sync tips

  • Keep Wi-Fi and Bluetooth enabled on both phones.
  • Keep phones near each other while connecting.
  • If the connection drops, keep scoring. Estate Golf saves scores locally and syncs when phones reconnect.
  • If reconnect does not happen automatically, stop Nearby and start a new Nearby attempt.

Scorecards and history

Rounds are stored locally on your device. Completed rounds appear in Rounds history. You can delete a saved round from the history list.
